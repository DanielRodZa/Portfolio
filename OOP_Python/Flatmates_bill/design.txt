Title: Flatmates Bill
Description:
    An app that gets as input amount of a bill for a particular period and the days each of the flatmates stayed
    in the house for that period and return how much each flatmate has to pay. It also generates a PDF report stating the
    names of the flatmates, the period, and how much each of them had to pay.

Objects:
    Bill:
        amount
        period

    Flatmate:
        name
        days_in_house

        pays(bill)

    PDFReport:
        filename
        generate(flatmate1, flatmate2, bill)
